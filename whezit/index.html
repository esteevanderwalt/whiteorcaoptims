From the maker

Why I built Whezit

I kept losing things I found on my phone. A recipe on Instagram, a podcast someone mentioned, an article I wanted to read later — gone, buried in browser tabs or forgotten in a sea of screenshots. Every "save for later" tool I tried was designed for desktops or wanted me to create yet another account.

So I built Whezit: a dead-simple way to save anything from any app using the share button you already use. No cloud, no account, no setup. Just tap share, pick Whezit, and it's saved. I built it for myself first, and now I'm sharing it with anyone who's tired of losing the things they meant to keep.

Your phone is where you discover things. Whezit is where they stay.

Most save-later tools are designed for browsers. Whezit is designed for the phone in your hand — where you actually find things worth keeping.
